Custom Duels Trial Moderator Guide

Welcome to the Custom Duels moderation team! This guide will help you through your trial phase.

Welcome New Moderators

This guide will be of great use in your journey to pass your trial phase. Please refer back to it when you feel you've forgotten any information. If anything is missing, don't hesitate to contact Cyan so the document can be updated.

First Steps

  1. Read [#staff-rules]. These are new rules that every staff member in Custom Duels must follow, in addition to the regular rules.
  2. Drop your time zone in [#timezones].
  3. Ensure you're in both the [support server] and tutor server.
  4. Follow this guide and you're all set!

Moderator Expectations

Punishments

The most obvious part of your job is to punish rule-breakers. All punishments are stated in [#punishments].

Commands to Punish Players

  • Ban players in-game: .ban (player) (rule) (reason) (e.g., .ban cya7n g-1 Circle) OR /ban (follow the prompt on Discord). Use the Discord command for time-bans or to ban users not in-game. Log EVERY ban using /log create (follow prompt) or /log add if a log already exists. Always log with downloaded attachments, not links like Medal or Streamable.
  • Kick players in-game: .kick (player) (reason) OR /kick (follow prompt) on Discord.
  • Mute players in-game: .mute (player)
  • Request tournament blacklists: /tournament blacklist (follow prompt) on Discord.
  • Ban players on Discord: You don't have this permission.
  • Kick players on Discord: (rarely used) /kick (follow prompt)
  • Mute players on Discord: /mute (follow prompt)
  • Warn players on Discord: /warn user (follow prompt)

To revoke punishments, add "un" before commands (e.g., /tournament unblacklist, /unmute).

Extra Information

  • If unsure about banning a player (e.g., uncertain if they're exploiting), use [#ban-discussion]. Ping @staff and provide evidence.
  • If a player's logs are corrupt and unsaved, you may unban them if requested.
  • Exploiting in other games doesn't warrant a Custom Duels ban.
  • When banning exploiters, specify the exploit (e.g., "Circle" not "Exploiting").
  • Don't re-open ban discussions. Direct users to open admin tickets for false bans or use the appeal system.
  • Check blacklist/whitelist status in [#game-bans] by typing the user's ID or username.
  • Other moderation logs are in the Logs category.

Focus Groups

Choose your focus group with /staff focus in Discord. Request changes in the first half of the week.

Game-Focused Moderators (Weekly)

  • ≥5 hours of game time
  • ≥2 tickets completed
  • Voted on all GAME appeals (skipping ≤2 is fine)

Discord-Focused Moderators (Weekly)

  • ≥1,000 Discord messages
  • ≥2 tickets completed
  • Voted on all DISCORD appeals (skipping ≤2 is fine)

Combo-Focused Moderators (Weekly)

  • ≥4 hours of game time
  • ≥500 Discord messages
  • Voted on ALL appeals (skipping ≤2 is fine)

Note: Trial mods don't have appeal access and aren't required to vote on them.

Consequences

Staff members who break rules receive strikes (unless the rule is severe). Three strikes within two weeks or fifteen within a year results in demotion. Strikes are removed after one year.

Admins+ impose strikes if:

  • A staff member fails activity requirements
  • A staff member breaks a rule that would warrant a warning for regular members

Staff are held to higher standards. What might be a time-ban for regular users could be a permanent, non-appealable ban for staff.

Failing all three weekly activity requirements results in three strikes (demotion). Contact managers if you believe a strike is unfair.

Going on Leave

Staff may take two-week leaves using /staff leave. Request leaves in the first half of the week, but return anytime. Limit one leave per month—excessive leaves may result in demotion.

NSFW Guidelines

NSFW content (profile pictures, images, videos, GIFs, etc.) is strictly prohibited.

Ban-Worthy

  • Full NSFW (nudity/pornography)

Warning-Worthy

  • Implicit NSFW (implies sexual activity without nudity, sexualized content)

Verbal Warning-Worthy

  • Mildly implicit NSFW (potentially sexual but ambiguous content)

When uncertain about content, consult other staff. If no consensus is reached, ping HR with the image and message link.

Appeals (MOD+)

Vote on appeals in [#appeals]. Read each thoroughly and act accordingly. Check ban logs with /log get (follow prompt).

[#punishments] shows if bans are appealable, but you must verify false bans and case severity. Vote honestly—you won't be punished for unpopular opinions.
